
## Binary, Decimal, and Hex

Complete the following problems:

* Convert `11001111` binary

    to hex: `1100 = C`, `1111 = F` => `0b11001111 = 0xCF`

    to decimal: `0b11001111=2^7+2^6+0+0+2^3+2^2+2^1+2^0=128+64+0+0+8+4+2+1=207`

* Convert `4C` hex

    to binary: `0b1001100`

    to decimal: `76`


* Convert `68` decimal

    to binary: `0b1000100`

    to hex: `0x44`
